## What is Saltatio Mortis?
**Saltatio Mortis** is a web-based, turn-based text role-playing game where the player interacts with non-player characters *(NPCs)*, completes missions, and uses strategic items to progress in the game. The system includes advanced management of characters, items, missions, and NPC behavioral strategies.

## Why did we make it?
The project was born as a group educational exercise, but the choice of a role-playing game proved ideal because it requires building a simulated world with personalized rules and behaviors—almost like a coded transposition of reality.

We found this to be a very valuable exercise that allowed us primarily to develop **logical skills**, **abstraction**, and **collaboration**, and to mature an approach to constant problem-solving using the Object-Oriented Programming *(OOP)* paradigm.

## Features
- Dynamic management of `characters` with attributes such as health, attack, and dexterity.
- `Items` with extendable behavior via derived classes.
- `Missions` structured with `environments`, enemies, and rewards.
- Configurable NPC `strategies` to define aggressive, defensive, or balanced play styles.
- Serialization and deserialization of data using *Marshmallow* for easy saving and loading.
